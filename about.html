My name is Kalima and that's my school project

My vision

Is a world where all animals are treated with compassion and respect, protected from cruelty and exploitation, and have access to proper care and medical treatment. We advocate for their welfare, raise awareness, and work on policies to promote their well-being.

My goal

Every year, about 100,000 stray animals are caught in Kazakhstan. About 50% of captured animals are euthanized. In this regard, the goal of our foundation is to help animals raise money and not leave them in poverty, hunger and give them a safe place.
